What is pilates


Pilates is a type of mind-body exercise developed in the early 20th century by German physical trainer Joseph Pilates. It improves flexibility, builds strength and develops control and endurance in the entire body.


Pilates can be practice on a mat or on a Reformer. It puts emphasis on alignment, breathing, developing a strong core, and improving coordination and balance.

Why us

Our classes


We have Reformer Pilates (machine) classes that provide resistance with its springs, leverage and body weight. And mat classes, where you are lying down on a mat and perform exercises with low impact and look for strengthening your muscles and achieving endurance movements.


Pilates' system allows for different exercises to be modified in range of difficulty from beginner to advanced. You can choose from HIIT pilates, pregnancy, group or one on one sessions.
